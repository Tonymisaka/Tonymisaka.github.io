[1.15.2022] 去Elver公园玩滑雪板！
Have fun playing snow board in the Elver Park!

终于出家门了。第一次进滑雪场，开心，但滑雪板不太好控制。首滑不知道情况，比较大胆，啥都没想就下去了。结果没想到速度这么快，在中途打了好几个转， 跟过山车没什么区别，吓死我了。第二次花了好长时间才敢鼓起勇气继续向下滑的。。。
Finally out of the house. First time in ski resort, happy, but skis not easy to control. The first slide did not know the situation, rather bold, did not think anything down. Results did not expect the speed so fast, in the middle of several turns, with no difference from the roller coaster, scared me to death. The second one took a long time to pluck up the courage to keep sliding...
